What this number says

The marketing for actively-managed funds promises alpha — outperformance over the index. Over 10 years, 73% of Indian active equity funds underperform their benchmark (SPIVA India 2024). You're paying premium fees for a coin-flip on underperformance.

An index fund with 0.2% TER on the same ₹10K SIP would compound to roughly the same as a 1% TER active fund that beats the index by 0.8%. The active fund needs to be exceptional just to break even.

The math doesn't care about the brochure. It cares about basis points.
