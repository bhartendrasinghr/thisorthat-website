How this maths works

For each goal we inflate the target to the future-rupee amount, subtract what your already-saved corpus grows into, and solve for the monthly SIP that fills the gap. Then we sum every goal's SIP and compare against (income − expenses) — your investable. This page never sends your numbers anywhere — it saves them in your browser only.

The uncomfortable bit
—

Most people plan goals one at a time — retirement here, the down payment there, the kid's college "we'll figure it out". Each calculator says "yes, doable" in isolation. Stack them up and the math usually doesn't fit.

That's not a failure — that's the planning. A plan that doesn't make trade-offs isn't a plan. It's a wishlist.
