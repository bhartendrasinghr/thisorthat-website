Why we're fussy about registration

India has two kinds of money help worth having: SEBI-registered investment advisers, who are paid by you and owe their advice to you, and AMFI-registered mutual fund distributors, who help you invest and earn a commission from the fund house, disclosed upfront. Both are regulated, accountable and checkable. Everyone else with a hot tip is entertainment, at best.

ThisOrThat is a media brand, not a SEBI-registered investment adviser or a mutual fund distributor, and this page is not investment advice. We only make introductions to registered professionals; any engagement, fees and advice are strictly between you and them, so verify their registration before proceeding. Your details are used solely to make the introduction and are never sold or added to marketing lists.
