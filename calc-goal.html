How this maths works

We take your goal (in today's rupees), inflate it for the years you've got, subtract whatever your existing corpus will grow into, and solve for the monthly SIP that fills the gap. If the required SIP is more than 50% of your take-home, that's a signal: the goal needs more time, less ambition, or a different income.

The uncomfortable bit
—

Most "goal calculators" online tell you a comfortable SIP and stop there. They forget that the goal will be more expensive by the time you get to it, and that you have an income, not a magic wallet.

This one shows the honest SIP, the share of your income it would eat, and what changes if you give the goal more time or less ambition.
