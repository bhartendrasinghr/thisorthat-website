The part the grid hides

Your target will shrink on the way. ₹1 Cr arriving in - buys what - buys today, at 6% inflation. Still worth the climb, just know what's at the top.

To arrive with today's buying power intact: aim for - instead, which needs - a month in a large cap fund at 12%. Bigger number, same dream, honest maths.
