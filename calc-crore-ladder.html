The ladder's secret
The first crore is the slowest. Every crore after it arrives faster.

Keep the ₹1-crore SIP running past your tenure and watch the gaps shrink. That is compounding taking over the climb.

The part the ladder hides

Your rung will shrink on the way up. ₹1 Cr arriving in - buys what - buys today, at 6% inflation. Still worth the climb, just know what's at the top.
