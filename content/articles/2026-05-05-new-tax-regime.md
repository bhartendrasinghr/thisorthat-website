The new tax regime is simpler. That's its only real selling point, and for most people, that's enough.

## The actual decision

Old regime: lower headline rates if you claim deductions. New regime: lower flat rates with almost no deductions allowed.

If your annual deductions are under ₹3 lakh, the new regime usually wins. Above that, do the math both ways.

## Who benefits

- Salaried with no HRA, no home loan, no 80C investments beyond EPF, **new regime, easy win**
- Home loan + 80C maxed + HRA, **old regime, still better**
- Senior citizens with medical premiums, usually old, by a bit

## The thing nobody mentions

The new regime is structurally designed to win over time. Each budget tweaks slabs upward; deductions get harder. If you're under 40 and starting your career, defaulting to new and forgetting about it is rarely the wrong choice.
