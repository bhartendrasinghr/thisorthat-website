> Your temperament, not your spreadsheet, decides your money, and Kabir and Rahim knew that before Excel did.

## What they care about

Vinayak Sapre is a financial coach who reached financial freedom in 2011 and has argued ever since that money is mostly a temperament problem, not a products or Excel-sheet one. He leans on Kabir and Rahim's dohas to link old wisdom to modern decisions, reframes retirement as FILE, financial independence with life at ease, and warns that chasing the biggest returns usually ends in disappointment.

A listener comes away seeing a good advisor as a filter, a saadhu jaisa soop that keeps only what belongs in your portfolio, and remembering that the point is to enjoy your money, not just accumulate it, even if you are starting late.
