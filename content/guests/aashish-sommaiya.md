## What they care about

Aashish Sommaiya, equity partner at WhiteOak Capital Group and CEO of WhiteOak Capital Asset, keeps pulling investors back to a simpler first question: not which fund to buy, but why you are investing at all. His argument is that the goal decides the vehicle, that there is no single perfect road to wealth, and that staying inside regulated products beats chasing whatever the market is loudest about.

What a listener takes away is that outcomes turn less on macroeconomics and more on how you handle your own emotions.
