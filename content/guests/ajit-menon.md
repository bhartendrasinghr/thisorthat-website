> Everyone can be happy; it is the constant chase to be happier where the trouble usually starts.

## What they care about

Ajit Menon, former CEO of PGIM Mutual Fund, brings 30 years to one reframe: retirement planning is really about financial freedom, security and peace of mind, and that shift changes the decisions you make. His core theme is happy versus happier, the idea that the constant chase to feel better is where the trouble usually begins.

He walks through all seven asset classes and warns against concentrating in one while ignoring economic cycles, favouring simple rules like buying at a discount and keeping things uncomplicated with Balance Advantage Funds. For anyone investing on a limited income, he is honest about sequence of return risk and realistic expectations, so the anxiety that stops people from planning does not get the last word.
