## In their own words

> Chasing returns is the wrong start: wealth is psychology, discipline and a plan you keep raising.

## What they care about

Pooja Bhinde is a Certified Financial Planner who treats wealth as a question of psychology, discipline and planning rather than hot tips or the next product. She keeps returning to the step-up SIP, where you raise your contribution as your income grows, and to the wider plan around it: budgeting, cash flow, a contingency fund, insurance and staying clear of debt traps.

Her stories run from a house help's SIP to security for senior citizens, and she is firm that women belong in a household's money decisions. A listener leaves seeing wealth-building as a habit almost anyone can start, not a game reserved for the rich.
