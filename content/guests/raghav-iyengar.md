CEO of Axis Mutual Fund. Veteran of the Indian asset management industry.

## In their own words

> Staying steady when markets swing is a skill you practice, not a temperament you were born with.

## What they care about

Raghav Iyengar brings over three decades in the mutual fund industry, and what he cares about most is how investor behavior has shifted: from short-term trading to goal-based investing.

His discipline is concrete: aim to save at least 30% of your monthly income, build an emergency fund before you reach for anything riskier, stick with regulated investment managers, and get honest about your own risk profile. The idea that stays with a listener is that staying calm through volatile markets is a practiced skill, not a personality trait, and it matters as much as which fund you pick.
