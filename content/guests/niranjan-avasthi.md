## In their own words

> Behaviour beats the best fund: knowing when to exit matters more than owning the top performer.

## What they care about

Niranjan Avasthi, Head of Product at Edelweiss Mutual Fund, is out to make fund investing feel less overwhelming. Faced with 36-plus categories, he hands listeners two plain frameworks: T.I.M.E. to choose your first funds, and F.I.T. to judge the fund house, team and track record instead of chasing whatever performed well recently.

His recurring point is that behaviour beats performance: rolling returns tell a more honest story than cherry-picked point-to-point numbers, and knowing when to exit and staying steady matter more than owning the single best fund. A listener leaves with a simple 80:20 equity-debt starting formula and a clear-eyed take on SIPs, NFOs, gold and thematic funds.
